Change the Way You Play the Game

Introducing a new way to track your pickup basketball stats that's fast, easy, and free.

What is it?

From an idea created by Taylor Lark, we began as a google spreadsheet to track pickup basketball games in Central Oregon. If you're like us, advanced analytics are a huge part of what makes basketball fun. However, pickup games are not timed and do not have people on the sidelines to record stats. We have created a way that allows users to enter in stats by watching a playback in real time. Those stats are then converted into analytics based primarily off of a 75 PER. Players can track a league of their friends, look at rankings, and see leaderboards— all within a few minutes. This site is currently in production and will one day allow input directly from users, but for now, check out already inputted stats by clicking here.
