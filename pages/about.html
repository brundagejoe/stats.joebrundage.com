About Us

How do we work?

First and foremost, this website is still being built. Once completed, a user can create a free account where they can track their own league. A section is in development where users can enter what happens during a game quickly, and with that alone stats are developed. Multiple accounts can be associated with a league, so all friends can see their stats and track records.

Statistics are an essential part of sports. They allow us to see things that we are not able to from pure observation. Our hope is that by tracking the statistics, you will be able to notice things about your ability to play basketball, focus on improvement in the right areas, and have fun while doing it.
